
       This function will copy a certificate into a PKCS 11 token specified
       by a URL. Valid flags to mark the certificate:
       GNUTLS_PKCS11_OBJ_FLAG_MARK_TRUSTED,
       GNUTLS_PKCS11_OBJ_FLAG_MARK_SENSITIVE,
       GNUTLS_PKCS11_OBJ_FLAG_MARK_PRIVATE, GNUTLS_PKCS11_OBJ_FLAG_MARK_CA,
       GNUTLS_PKCS11_OBJ_FLAG_MARK_ALWAYS_AUTH.
