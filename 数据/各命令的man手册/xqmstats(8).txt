
       xqmstat queries the kernel for the XFS Quota Manager dquot
       statistics.  It displays:
           ·      Reclaims
           ·      Missed reclaims
           ·      Dquot dups
           ·      Cache misses
           ·      Cache hits
           ·      Dquot wants
           ·      Shake reclaims
           ·      Inact reclaims
